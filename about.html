My Life

Here's a little bit about me!

Growing Up

Food was never a huge focus for me. I was lucky enough to have a mom who was kind enough to pack a lunch for me most days of the week.

I would occasionally indulge in some pizza or tacos at the lunch line, but for the most part, my lunches were home-cooked.

Now

I'm not a very good cook, but I am capable of making a few staple recipes to keep myself alive and healthy. However, I do enjoy baking!
