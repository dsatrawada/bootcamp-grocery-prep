Oatmeal

Servings

4

Ingredients

  • 2 cup instant oats
  • 3 cup milk or water

Instructions

  1. Add instant oats into a microwave-safe bowl.
  2. Pour milk or water into the bowl and stir.
  3. Microwave for 60 to 90 seconds.
  4. Let the oatmeal cool for two minutes.
  5. Enjoy!
