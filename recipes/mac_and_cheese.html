Macaroni and Cheese

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of elbow macaroni
  • 1/2 cup of sharp cheddar
  • 6 cups of water
  • 2 - 4 tbsp of margarine or butter
  • 1/4 cup of milk

Instructions

  1. Boil the water in a saucepan.
  2. Stir in the macaroni and cook for 7 to 8 minutes, or until tender.
  3. Drain the water and return the macaroni to the pan.
  4. Add in the margarine or butter, milk, and cheese.
  5. Stir and enjoy!
