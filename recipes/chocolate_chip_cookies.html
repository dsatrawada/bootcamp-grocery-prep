Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar (packed)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teapoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 4 oz semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 4 oz dark chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the sugars, salt, and butter.
  2. Whisk in the egg and vanilla and beat until fully combined.
  3. Sift in the flour and baking soda and fold the mixture with a spatula.
  4. Fold in the chocolate chunks, then chill the dough for at least 30 minutes.
  5. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees farenheit and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  6. Scoop the dough with an ice-cream scoop onto the baking sheet.
  7. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the edges have started to brown.
  8. Let the cookies cool and serve!
