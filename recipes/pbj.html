Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of sandwich bread of your choice
  • 2 tablespoons of peanut butter
  • 2 tablespoons of jam or jelly of your choice

Instructions

  1. Spread the peanut butter on one slice of bread.
  2. Soread the jam or jelly on the other slice of bread.
    3. .
  3. Put the two slices of bread together to form your sandwich.
  4. Cut off the crusts (optional).
  5. Enjoy!
